About Tinkercad

To create their 3D Models, the kids taking these classes use a free, online 3D design tool called Tinkercad. Tinkercad is easy to use for even our youngest students and requires no prior design experience. First time 3D designers can create and print their designs within minutes! And it's free! Join now!
